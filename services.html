Work Flow

01. Consult
We will discuss your needs, your ideas and your expectations while giving you a glimpse of how adorable and charming we are in person.
02. Brainstorm
We will gather the nerds in our nerd lair and not come out until we have something that will blow you away.
03. Design
Once the idea is approved, we will design it and make it even more awesome with interactive and responsive design as well as a look and feel that will make it stand out from the competition.
04. Development
We take the design and make it a reality with code so beautiful, secure and well written it may make you cry . . maybe that's just me, though. I get emotional. Don't judge.

Through every phase of development we will extensively test and re-test until we know for certain that whatever you need functions perfectly without errors or mistakes. Don't get me wrong, we make them (it's impossible not to), but we also find and fix them before you see it.
06. Launch
We spend time to train and discuss with you the in-and-outs of everything we have done for you, and then we launch it to the public so everyone can see how awesome and great you and your business or product is.
