A few words about us

Who are you?
NERDISM - (noun) The practices or behaviors of nerds; nerdiness.
We are a creative design and development firm in Dallas, TX, founded by people who believe that functionality and beauty can be integrated to make something special we can use to further your business or personal brand.
What we do?
We take your idea or existing business and bring it to the world in way that grabs the attention of the consumer and magnifies your product so you can increase profitability.
We believe that humor combined with amazing design and a creative use of technology can make complicated ideas more understandable and products more fun.
Why we do it?
We like money and paying our bills on time, and doing it surrounded by people we like and doing things we really love to do.
That's probably oversimplified, but we do this because we are good at it, and we make a difference in each others lives as well as our clients. We are incredibly lucky people.
